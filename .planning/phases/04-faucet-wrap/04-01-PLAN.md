      - "1000" or 1000 -> 1000
      - 2_000_000 -> 1_000_000 (clamped to the per-call cap)

      - error message matching a revert / "exceeds"/ over-cap -> "Claim failed — amount may exceed the 1,000,000-per-call limit, or this token's mint is restricted."
      - user-rejected ("User rejected"/ "denied") -> "You declined the wallet prompt."
      - anything else -> "Could not claim test tokens — please try again."
