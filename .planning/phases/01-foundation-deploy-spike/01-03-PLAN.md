## Phase Goal

**As a** bounty judge, **I want to** open a live public Sepolia dApp URL that is cross-origin
isolated and connect my wallet behind a Sepolia network guard, **so that** the deploy / SSR /
COOP-COEP risk is proven dead before any feature is built on top of it.

> Walking-skeleton slice 3 of 3: the live Vercel deploy + on-URL verification (the phase gate).

<objective>
Deploy `packages/nextjs` to a real, public Vercel URL and verify — on the live URL, not
localhost — that the walking skeleton holds: cross-origin isolation is on, the FHE provider
mounts client-only, the app is publicly reachable, and the Sepolia chain guard works.

Purpose: This is the phase's whole point (FND-04 + SUB-03) — retire the "works locally, breaks
on Vercel" risk (COOP/COEP + WASM/SSR) on the real URL. A header misconfig degrades to
single-threaded WASM rather than crashing (01-RESEARCH.md Pitfall 2), so the failure mode is
"slow + FND-04 unmet," caught here by the live console check.

Output: A live public Vercel URL proven cross-origin isolated, with wallet + chain guard working.
</objective>
